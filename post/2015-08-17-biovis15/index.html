Architecture and design paper in Biovis '15

17.08.2015 in publications using tags publications

Our paper detailing the architecture and design of Epiviz is now available on the BMC Bioinformatics site. It is published in a supplement containing a good number of interesting papers from the Biovis ‘15 conference held as a satellite meeting of the ISMB 2015 conference. We also presented our Nature Methods paper on Epiviz in the highlights track at ISMB. Slides for that talk are available on github.
